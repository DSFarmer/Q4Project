TOP 5 DESTINATIONS IN THE PHILIPPINES
PHOTO GALLERY


   Welcome to the Philippines, Pearl of the Orient Seas. It is an archipelago in Southeast Asia
   known for its beautiful destinations, crystal clear waters, abundant resources, and warm hospitality. The Philippines is heaven for travelers around the world!

   To get started, here are Top 5 Destinations that surely wont disappoint:


  • Coron
  • Siargao
  • Baguio
  • Bohol
  • Cebu

   Coron

  

   Coron, Palawan is well known for its:

  •    Beautiful limestone cliffs with many attractions and sights
  •    Diving areas with vibrant coral reefs and more marine life
  •    Crystal-clear waters
  •    Japanese shipwrecks from World War II

   Siargao

  

   Siargao is also called the surfing capital of the Philippines. It's renowned for:

  •    Surfing with big waves
  •    Beautiful lagoons that are vibrant with marine life
  •    Cave exploration with many beautiful rocky formations
  •    Rock climbing, as Siargao is known to be a rocky-region

   Baguio

  

   Baguio is known to be the summer capital of the Philippines, it is known for:

  •    Cold climate, as Baguio resides as a mountain retreat
  •    The annual Panagbenga Festival, showing off many flower floats
  •    Strawberries in Baguio are known to be the best according to Filipino locals
  •    Baguio Night Market always has thrifty items and clothings to offer coming at you with cheap prices

   Bohol

  

   Bohol is one of the most popular places in the Philippines, as its known for:

  •    Chocolate Hills (cone-shaped hills that turn brown during dry season, making it look like chocolate)
  •    The famous "Tarsier", one of the worlds smallest animals, only found in the Philippines
  •    Loboc River, where you can cruise and explore this pristine river, known to be one of the cleanest

   Cebu

  

   Cebu is a city known for its rich history and culture, its renowned for:

  •    The Magellans cross, which was planted by our Spanish-Portguese colonizers, which is now a monument
  •    Moalboal, where tourists can swim with whale sharks
  •    The beautiful beaches of Mactan Island, known to be a place for island hopping
