   CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Juan Crisostomo y Magsalin Ibarra
  • Also known as "Ibarra" in the story.
  • Is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra who returned from Europe after 7 years of studying. He is also half Spanish.
  • Ibarra strives to fight for the right of the Filipinos, for he denies corruption and tedious ways.
  • He aims to establish a school to educate younglings and continue the good legacy of his father. Although, he encounters many obstacles throughout his journey in such corrupt society.
Kapitan Tiago
  • His full name is "Don Sant√≠ago de los Santos".
  • He is well-known in San Diego, popular among people especially the church and its friars.
  • Kapitan Tiago is a rich, influential, and powerful man. He is corrupt by using his wealthiness for his benefit.
  • Arranged a marriage with Don Rafael Ibarra to marry off his daughter, Maria Clara, to Crisostomo Ibarra.
Padre Damaso
  • Is a corrupt, greedy, abusive Francisan priest and former curate of San Diego.
  • He is soon to be revealed as the true father of Maria Clara from a forced "relationship" with Maria Clara's mother.
  • Has a superiority complex and wants the Filipinos all under control by the church.
Padre Salvi
  • He became assigned as the new curate of San Diego after Padre Damaso.
  • Alferez, the chief of civil guards, is his enemy.
  • Just like Padre Damaso, he is corrupt as he engages in manipulation and deception because of his high position. This allows him to exploit the Filipinos.
Anastacio
  • Also known as "Pilosopo Tasio" for being intellectual, or "Tasyong Baliw" if people don't see eye-to-eye with his ideologies.
  • He is a highly opinionated, educated, and open-minded man.
  • Because of his sarcasm and unordinary ideas, he is dismissed and deemed as a lunatic in their society.
